Welcome to the Breathing Exercise

Follow the steps below to begin your exercise:

  1. Enter the number of breathing cycles you want to complete.
  2. Click the "Start Breathing Exercise" button.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions for inhale and exhale.
  4. Try to blow out the candle during the exhale phase!

This exercise uses your microphone to detect your breath. Please ensure you grant microphone access when prompted.
